About:
Royal's House is a student accommodation provider that offers comfortable and affordable hostel accommodations for university students. Located in Kampar, Perak. Royal's House provides an ideal living space for students who are looking for a convenient and comfortable place to call home.

At Royal's House, students can choose from a variety of room types, including single, double, and shared rooms. Each room is fully furnished with a bed, desk, chair, wardrobe, and other necessary amenities to ensure that students have everything they need to study and relax.

The hostel is equipped with a range of facilities such as high-speed internet, laundry facilities, a common kitchen area, and a lounge area where students can socialize and unwind. Additionally, the hostel provides 24/7 security to ensure that students feel safe and secure at all times.

One of the key benefits of staying at Royal's House is its prime location. The hostel is situated close to UTAR, making it an ideal living space for students who want to be close to their academic institution. Additionally, the hostel is in a bustling area of the city, with easy access to public transportation, restaurants, cafes, and other amenities.

The team at Royal's House is dedicated to ensuring that students have a comfortable and enjoyable stay. The staff is friendly, approachable, and always ready to help students with any queries or concerns they may have.

In summary, Royal's House provides comfortable, safe, and affordable living space for university students. With its prime location, range of facilities, and friendly staff, it's no wonder that Royal's House is a top choice for students looking for quality accommodation.
